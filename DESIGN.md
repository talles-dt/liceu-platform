 
# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core visual characteristics of our design system, derived directly from our design tokens.

## Color Palette

Our system employs a **dark** color mode, providing a sophisticated and immersive user experience.

- **Primary Color**: `#004D32` - This is our brand's most distinctive color, used for key interactive elements and calls to action.
- **Secondary Color**: `#B5A642` - A supporting color for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
- **Tertiary Color**: Not explicitly defined in the tokens, using derived values from the system.
- **Neutral Color**: `#0D0D0D` - This serves as our base for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, ensuring readability and contrast in dark mode.

## Typography

Our typographic system is built on a clear hierarchy to ensure optimal readability and brand expression.

- **Headlines**: `notoSerif` - Chosen for its strong presence and classic readability in headings.
- **Body Text**: `workSans` - Provides excellent legibility for long-form content and general body text.
- **Labels**: `spaceGrotesk` - Selected for its modern and distinct character, suitable for labels and smaller interface text.

## Shape and Form

The physical characteristics of our UI components define their interaction and visual appeal.

- **Roundedness**: `0` (Sharp, angular) - Our components feature sharp, angular corners, contributing to a modern and precise aesthetic.

## Spacing

The whitespace in our design is intentionally managed to create visual harmony and define relationships between elements.

- **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - We utilize a normal level of whitespace, providing a balanced and comfortable visual density for users. This ensures elements are distinct without feeling sparse or overly crowded.
